Data Collection Consent

Get Focused monitors your typing and mouse patterns to detect cognitive fatigue. To improve the prediction model, we ask your permission to share anonymized behavioral metrics.

What is shared (anonymized)

  • Typing speed and rhythm patterns
  • Pause frequency and duration
  • Error rate (backspace frequency)
  • Mouse speed and idle time
  • Session duration
  • Fatigue prediction results

What is never collected

  • Actual typed characters or words
  • Passwords or form data
  • URLs or browsing history
  • Personal identifiers or names
  • Screen content or screenshots
You can change this at any time in the extension settings. The extension works fully without data sharing — it only affects whether anonymized metrics are sent to improve the model.
